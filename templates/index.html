Get Vaccinated
and Stay Free from COVID-19
through Immuno Shots.


Immuno Shots is a Web-based application which allows you to book your vaccination center and get Vaccinated in just one click. You can also see the nearby Vaccination centers through the map, and also book your appointment with our specialized doctors. You can also book an Ambulance if you have any emergency.





The Problem



  • In India, due to it's huge population it has became very difficult to vaccinate all the people by going to their houses or by calling them on Vaccination centers.

  • So, through Immuno Shots we provide vaccination booking services, so that each person gets the chance to get vaccinated as per first come first server basis.

  • People can also see their nearby Vaccination centers as per the data collected from our map which is then displayed to their screen.

  • If their is any serious case, then people can book an appointment and can have a one on one video call consulting with our specialized doctors and can also book an Ambulance in any emergency or to reach the respective centers.
